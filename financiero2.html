Data & BI
BI Data Analyst / BI Specialist
FocusTransforms raw business data into actionable intelligence through dashboards, reports, and data models that drive strategic decisions.
TasksBuilding KPI dashboards in Power BI or Tableau, writing SQL queries, designing data pipelines, and presenting findings to stakeholders.
SkillsSQL, Power BI / Tableau, DAX, Python or R basics, data warehousing concepts, strong visual communication.
GoalProvide management with a clear, real-time picture of business performance to enable faster, data-driven decisions.

Online Financial & Accounting Assistant
FocusSupports day-to-day financial and accounting operations remotely, ensuring transactions are recorded accurately and deadlines are met.
TasksInvoice processing, bank reconciliation, accounts payable/receivable management, data entry in ERP systems, and supporting month-end close.
SkillsExcel, ERP/accounting software (SAP, QuickBooks, Odoo), attention to detail, time management, digital collaboration tools.
GoalKeep financial records clean, current, and compliant while freeing senior staff to focus on analysis and strategy.

Administrative Accounting & Finance Officer
FocusBridges administrative management with financial control, overseeing bookkeeping, reporting, and budget monitoring across the organization.
TasksGeneral ledger maintenance, preparation of financial statements, cash flow monitoring, payroll support, supplier and client account management.
SkillsDouble-entry bookkeeping, IFRS/GAAP knowledge, advanced Excel, ERP systems, organizational skills, regulatory awareness.

Cybersecurity
Information Security Analyst
FocusProtects the organization's information assets by identifying vulnerabilities, monitoring threats, and implementing security controls.
TasksVulnerability assessments, security audits, incident response, firewall and IDS management, security awareness training, and policy enforcement.
SkillsSIEM tools, network security, ISO 27001, penetration testing basics, risk assessment frameworks, CISSP or Security+ certifications.
GoalReduce the organization's attack surface and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of all information systems.

Governance
GRC Technician (Governance, Risk & Compliance)
FocusEnsures the organization operates within regulatory frameworks, manages enterprise risk, and maintains governance standards.
TasksRisk register management, compliance gap analysis, internal controls testing, policy drafting, regulatory reporting, and audit support.
SkillsRisk frameworks (COSO, ISO 31000), regulatory knowledge (GDPR, SOX), GRC platforms (Archer, ServiceNow), audit methodologies.
GoalAlign business operations with legal, regulatory, and ethical standards while proactively managing enterprise risks.

Security Ops
SOC Analyst (Security Operations Center)
FocusMonitors, detects, investigates, and responds to cybersecurity threats in real time from within a Security Operations Center.
TasksAlert triage and investigation, log analysis, threat hunting, incident containment, escalation procedures, and threat intelligence consumption.
SkillsSIEM (Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel), EDR tools, MITRE ATT&CK framework, network forensics, scripting (Python/Bash), CompTIA CySA+.
GoalDetect and neutralize threats as early as possible to minimize damage and downtime to the business.

C-Suite
Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
FocusLeads the entire financial strategy of the organization, overseeing all financial operations, reporting, planning, and capital allocation.
TasksSetting financial strategy, investor relations, capital structure decisions, M&A oversight, board reporting, and leading finance, accounting, and treasury teams.
SkillsStrategic leadership, deep financial expertise, M&A experience, stakeholder communication, risk management, CPA/CFA qualifications typical.
GoalMaximize the financial health and long-term value of the company while ensuring transparency and trust with all stakeholders.
