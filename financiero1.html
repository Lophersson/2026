FP&A Analyst

An FP&A (Financial Planning & Analysis) Analyst is a corporate finance professional responsible for budgeting, forecasting, and reporting to guide strategic business decisions. They analyze financial data, monitor performance against goals, and create financial models. These analysts bridge financial data with operational strategy, often specializing in sales revenue, costs, or company-wide performance.

Career Path

FP&A Analysts often start in general accounting or junior analyst roles before specializing in FP&A. Career progression typically moves from Analyst to Senior Analyst, FP&A Manager, Director of Finance, and potentially Chief Financial Officer (CFO).
