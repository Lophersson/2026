FP&A vs Controller

An FP&A analyst focuses on forward-looking strategy, budgeting, and forecasting to drive business decisions, while a Controller manages historical financial accuracy, compliance, and accounting operations. FP&A analysts analyze trends for growth, whereas Controllers ensure data integrity and internal controls.
